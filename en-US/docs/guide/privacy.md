# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2026

grekt ("we", "us") is a source available project. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## What we collect

- Email and username from GitHub/Google when you log in
- Artifacts you publish (name, version, content)

## Why

- Authentication and account identification
- Associating published artifacts with their authors

## What we don't do

- Sell your data
- Track you across websites
- Send marketing emails

## Data storage

- Hosted on Supabase (see their [privacy policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- Artifacts stored on Cloudflare R2

## Delete your account

You can delete your account from your account settings. If you have any issues, contact us at contact@grekt.com.

This policy may change. Check this page for updates.
