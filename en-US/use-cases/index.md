# Use cases

grekt solves different problems depending on your role and scale. These use cases walk through real-world scenarios with practical examples you can follow along.

## For everyone

| Use case | Summary |
|----------|---------|
| [Multitool workflow](./multi-tool-workflow) | Use any combination of AI tools with a single source of truth |
| [Publishing and sharing](./publishing-and-sharing) | Share your best agents, skills, and rules with the community |
| [Composable artifact architecture](./composable-artifact-architecture) | Split artifacts into consumer and maintainer layers for different audiences |
| [Security first consumption](./security-first-consumption) | Scan and verify third party artifacts before they touch your codebase. **Enforce thresholds in CI/CD** to block risky artifacts |

## For teams

| Use case | Summary |
|----------|---------|
| [Team onboarding](./team-onboarding) | Get every developer running with identical AI context in seconds |
| [Context management at scale](./context-management-at-scale) | Keep 20+ artifacts without polluting your AI tool's context |
| [Private registry for enterprises](./private-registry) | Use your own GitHub/GitLab infrastructure as a self-hosted artifact registry |

## For open source

| Use case | Summary |
|----------|---------|
| [Open source AI context](./open-source-ai-context) | Ship AI context alongside your code so contributors are productive immediately |
